    Professional Profile

    Seasoned clinical data professional with 12+ years of experience managing and engineering data for public health initiatives and clinical trials across pharmaceutical organizations and CROs.

    Specialized in ensuring data integrity while meeting rigorous timelines and compliance standards. Proven leader with exceptional communication skills and a passion for mentoring team members. Recognized for balancing collaborative teamwork with self-directed project execution to drive successful study outcomes.

    Currently serving as an FSP Clinical Data Manager/Engineer, building on previous roles as Data Manager and Data Analyst to deliver comprehensive data solutions throughout the clinical research lifecycle.

    Key Strengths

    Clinical Data Systems: Advanced proficiency in industry-leading platforms like Medidata RAVE, Veeva Clinical, and Elluminate for end-to-end data management.
    Regulatory Compliance: Deep understanding of CDISC standards (CDASH,SDTM and ADaM) and clinical trial regulations (ICH-GCP, 21 CFR Part 11).
    Data Visualization: Designed interactive dashboards and reports using Tableau, Power BI, and R Shiny to drive data-driven decision-making.
    ETL & Data Pipelines: Built and optimized data workflows with Veeva CDB, Elluminate, SQL, Databricks, and Apache Spark.
    Clinical Programming: Developed custom scripts and tools in R, Python, and SAS for data transformation, analysis, and quality control.
    Cloud & Big Data: Leveraged AWS cloud services for scalable data solutions and storage.
    Web Development: Applied front-end skills (HTML/CSS/JavaScript) to create user-friendly data interfaces.
    Data Quality: Implemented robust validation frameworks to ensure compliance and accuracy in clinical datasets.
    Problem-Solving: Resolved complex data discrepancies through root-cause analysis and cross-functional collaboration.
    Communication: Translated technical insights into actionable recommendations for stakeholders across functions.
